The event (lightning strike) should be triggered by a mouse click from the user.

When the event is triggered, a lightning strike should spawn, affecting all blocks in a 7 block radius (circular ish). All trees should turn into stumps, and any Dude (full or not full) should turn into the Entity called FairyHunter.

3. what the affected entities should do
The FairyHunter Entity should turn hostile and target the Fairy Entities.

4. what the new entity is and how it should behave
The HunterDestroyer should target the Fairy Hunters (once they exist) and attempt to destroy them.
